Strategy

A straightforward flow that turns everyday inputs into connected, searchable knowledge.

1) Capture

Add notes, images, voice memos and videos. PData accepts multiple formats so you can capture naturally.

2) Understand

PData extracts subject–predicate–object triples and builds a graph representation optimized for exploration.

3) Explore

Search, filter and navigate relationships. Reveal themes and connections you might otherwise miss.

4) Act

Turn insights into action with dashboards and summaries that help with decisions and recall.
